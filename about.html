Our Vision

Amplifying voices. Connecting continents. Inspiring generations.

At Les Éditions du Schabel Canada, we believe that a book is more than a physical object: it is a living force, a vessel of memory, and a driver of transformation.

  • A Witness of History – Preserves journeys, struggles, legacies, and knowledge.
  • A Cry of Hope – Amplifies marginalized voices and turns silence into dignity.
  • A Tool for Social Transformation – Challenges, questions, and enlightens.
  • A Universal Forum for Dialogue – Bilingual approach takes stories beyond borders.
  • An Invisible School – Nurtures critical thinking and opens paths to action.
  • A Living Legacy – Publishing plants seeds that keep bearing fruit.

Who We Are

Les Éditions du Schabel Canada is a bilingual publishing house (French & English) based in Ontario, Canada. We are the natural extension of a strategic partnership with Les Éditions du Schabel (Cameroon), building an editorial bridge between Africa, its diasporas, and the world.

A House at the Crossroads of Cultures
We aim to be: a space of memory, a citizen forum, and a cultural bridge linking Africa, Europe, and the Americas.

Les Éditions du Schabel Canada est une maison d’édition bilingue (FR/EN) en Ontario. Prolongement d’un partenariat stratégique avec Les Éditions du Schabel (Cameroun), nous bâtissons un pont éditorial entre l’Afrique, ses diasporas et le monde.

Une maison au carrefour des cultures
Un espace de mémoire, une tribune citoyenne et une passerelle culturelle entre l’Afrique, l’Europe et l’Amérique.
